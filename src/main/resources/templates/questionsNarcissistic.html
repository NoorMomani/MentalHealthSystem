1. Do you experience an exaggerated sense of self-importance that frequently involves the need to exaggerate your talents or accomplishments?

2. Do you believe you are special and unique and can only be understood by, or should associate with, other special or high-status people or institutions?

3. Do you find that you constantly have a willingness to take advantage of others to achieve your own goals?

4. Do you require excessive admiration from others?

5. Are you preoccupied with fantasies of unlimited success, power, brilliance, beauty, or ideal love?

6. Do you have a sense of entitlement from others that involve unreasonable expectations of especially favorable treatment or automatic compliance with their expectations?

7. Do you find you are unwilling to recognize or identify with the feelings and needs of others?

8. Do others perceive you as arrogant or snobby?

9. Do you find that you are often envious of others and/or believe that others are envious of you?
